2. Why this protects you from "The Wall"
By grouping files into Tiers, you avoid the "Endless Update" trap.
You build a Pipeline once.
Any new "weird" image type that comes out in 2027 just becomes a new "Adapter" you plug into the existing Pipeline.
Your UI and PDF logic never have to change.

This is a significant strategic shift that actually simplifies your path to a finished product. By moving the "User" from the Employee to the Client (The Participant), you are moving the tool to the "Front of the House."
Instead of an employee fixing a mess that already arrived, you are providing a "Smart Gateway" that prevents the mess from ever being created. This is a much easier sell to a company because it directly improves the Customer Experience (CX).
1. The "Client-Facing" Strategy
In your manual, we will frame this as The Intelligent Upload Portal.
Current Reality: Client takes a blurry, sideways photo of a 401k statement on their iPhone (HEIC), uploads it, and it sits in a queue for 3 days before an employee realizes they can't open it.
Your Solution: The client goes to the company site. Your component opens. As they upload, the tool says: "Checking your file... Done! We've optimized this for our records. Please make sure the pages are in the right order." The result: The company receives a perfect, 150 DPI, upright PDF. The employee does zero work.

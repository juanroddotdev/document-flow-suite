## Adobe License Savings

Many organizations pay **$15–25 per user/month** for Adobe Acrobat Pro to fix document issues (rotation, HEIC conversion, multi-page PDF assembly).

### Example

- **100 employees** × $20/user/month ≈ **$24,000/year**
- If DocumentFlow Suite replaces Acrobat for **50%** of routine use → **$12,000/year** saved
- At **500 employees** → **$60,000/year** in potential savings

Actual numbers depend on your license count, usage patterns, and retention of Acrobat for advanced workflows.

## Labor Recovery

Employees spend time:

- Converting HEIC photos from phones to usable formats
- Rotating and re-saving PDFs
- Stitching multiple files into a single PDF

### Example

- **10 minutes/week** per employee on document triage
- 100 employees × 10 min × 52 weeks ≈ **867 hours/year**
- At $40/hour loaded cost → **$34,680/year** in recovered labor

DocumentFlow Suite centralizes these tasks in a fast, client-side tool—no waiting on Acrobat or manual steps.

## Break-Even

Combined savings (licenses + labor) often reach **$50k+ per year** for mid-size teams. A one-time implementation cost typically pays back within months.

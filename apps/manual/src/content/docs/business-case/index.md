## Cost Comparison: Adobe vs. DocumentFlow

Many organizations pay **$15–25 per user/month** for Adobe Acrobat Pro to fix document issues—rotation, HEIC conversion, multi-page PDF assembly.

### Example

- **100 employees** × $20/user/month ≈ **$24,000/year**
- If DocumentFlow Suite replaces Acrobat for **50%** of routine use → **$12,000/year** saved
- At **500 employees** → **$60,000/year** in potential savings

Actual numbers depend on your license count, usage patterns, and retention of Acrobat for advanced workflows.

## Workflow Impact

DocumentFlow reduces "back-and-forth" with clients over bad files:

- **Phase 1 (Internal Utility):** Employees fix files they've already received—no more waiting for clients to re-upload.
- **Phase 2 (Client Intake):** Fix files at the point of entry so bad uploads never reach your team.

Fewer NIGO submissions mean fewer support tickets, less re-work, and faster cycle times.
